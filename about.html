About

Hello! I am Steven/Xinqi Zhu. Now I am an undergraduate in Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU) in China, majoring in Computer Science.

I graduated from Shishi High School, Shishi Union Middle School and Fangcao Primary School. All of them are located in my hometown Chengdu Sichuan, China.

Though I'm not professional in building websites, I really enjoy it. It just like a programmer playing with colors and decorating a room. That's awesome! So I did it. Please feel free to tell me any thing that I can improve! I'll be very grateful!

Evangelion

A Japanese animation which inspired me with a new world. I love it for its stream of consciousness and unique construction of the world. Amanda Winn Lee(绫波丽) is my favorite character.

Warcraft

A Blizzard's successful game/story/world. I love it for its huge design and great characters. Maybe the age for this game has passed, the memories will never gone. (The movie is not bad anyway :-) .)

红楼梦

曹雪芹的史诗作品。书中对人物的刻画、命运的掌控、结构的把握，以及里面的诗歌，都美妙至极。富贵过的人很多，穷苦过的人很多，富贵过又穷苦过的人也不少，但又富过又穷过，最后将命运载入传世作品的人，少之又少。
